FAQ

Good Questions

Are the package prices fixed?

They're honest starting points. Your final per-person price depends on dates, group size, and accommodation level — and it's fully itemized in your proposal before you commit to anything.

How far ahead should we book?

Peak winter weeks genuinely sell out 6+ months ahead for groups. Summer is more forgiving, but the best chalets go early. Checking availability costs nothing.

What if none of the packages fit?

Then we build from scratch — the packages are just proven starting points. Tell us what you're picturing and we'll shape it.

Is there a planning fee?

No. Proposals are free and no-obligation; we're paid through our partner arrangements when you book.
