SMS Consent & Messaging Policy

Whistler Business Solutions — including our family-support phone lines such as the BirdNest Parent Helpline ("BabyLine") at 1-833-344-BABY (1-833-344-2229). Text messages described below are sent from 1-833-344-2229.

How you opt in

Consent to receive a text message is collected verbally, during a phone call that you initiate. When a caller asks about a product or resource, our assistant offers to text the relevant links using this script:

"Would you like me to text you a link to that, along with a link to our free companion app? You'll get one text message; message and data rates may apply, and you can reply STOP at any time."

A text message is sent only if the caller says yes. We never send messages to people who have not called us and expressly agreed during the call.

What we send

Exactly one text message per request, containing (1) a link to the product or resource the caller asked about and (2) a link to our free companion app for the helpline, as agreed verbally on the call. Nothing else is ever sent to the number — no campaigns, reminders, or follow-ups. Example message:

BabyLine: Ergobaby Omni 360 Carrier: https://api.whistlerbusinesssolutions.com/r/Ab12Cd34 Free app: https://api.whistlerbusinesssolutions.com/r/Ef56Gh78 Reply STOP to opt out.

We do not send recurring messages, marketing campaigns, or reminders. Typical volume is a single message per phone call, and only when requested. Message and data rates may apply.

How you opt out

Reply STOP to any message to stop receiving texts. Reply HELP for assistance, or contact us at support@whistlerbusinesssolutions.com.

Your privacy

Phone numbers are used solely to deliver the message you requested and are never sold or shared with third parties for their marketing. See our privacy policy for details.
