Maisie Schedules Integration

Maisie Schedules is a powerful Shopify app (in development) designed to turn your services into bookable events with customizable time slots. Perfect for talk therapists, it allows clients to book sessions directly through your Shopify store, with seamless calendar integration and automated reminders.

Benefits include:

  • Effortless scheduling for individual or group sessions
  • Secure payment processing via Shopify
  • Mobile-friendly booking interface
  • Integration with customer accounts for easy rebooking

Let us set up Maisie Schedules for your practice, so you can focus on helping your clients. Head to the Maisie Schedules app now.

Custom Shopify Store

We create fully customized Shopify stores that reflect your brand and meet the needs of your therapy practice. From session bookings to selling resources like workbooks or merchandise, your store will be a one-stop hub for your clients.
