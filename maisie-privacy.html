Maisie Schedules Privacy Policy (Draft)

Last Updated: February 22, 2025

Maisie Schedules is a Shopify app in development to help merchants manage order schedules. We process personal data from Shopify webhooks (e.g., ORDERS_CREATE) to provide this service.

What We Collect

  • Order Data: Order ID, items, etc., to track and schedule orders.
  • Shop Data: Shop domain to manage app functionality.

How We Use It

  • Data is used only to deliver scheduling features to merchants.
  • We do not sell or share customer data with third parties.

Consent

  • We process data as directed by merchants via Shopify settings.
  • Customer consent (e.g., marketing opt-ins) is respected per Shopifyâ€™s configuration.

Storage & Security

  • Data is stored temporarily for testing and encrypted in transit (HTTPS).
  • Formal retention and security policies are in development.
