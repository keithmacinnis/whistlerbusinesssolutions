FAQ

Good Questions

What does the planning service cost?

The trip plan is free and comes with no obligation. If you go ahead, we're paid through our partner arrangements — you don't pay us a planning fee.

How far ahead should we book?

For peak winter weeks (Christmas through spring break), group accommodation genuinely fills 6+ months out. Summer has more flexibility, but the best chalets go early. The sooner you send your brief, the more options you'll have.

Do you handle the actual bookings?

Yes. Once you approve the plan, we coordinate stays, transfers, dining, and activities with our Whistler partners, and you get one point of contact throughout.

What size of group do you work with?

Anything from a couple's escape to a 30+ person corporate outing. Bigger than that? Ask — we've got the partners for it.
