Why Whistler for Retreats & Outings

One Mountain Village, Every Kind of Getaway

Just under two hours from Vancouver along the Sea-to-Sky Highway, Whistler pairs a walkable, car-free village with world-class mountains — making it just as easy to gather a family as it is to reward a team. Great in every season.

❄️

Winter

Whistler Blackcomb — one of the largest ski resorts in North America — plus the record-setting Peak 2 Peak Gondola, snowshoeing, tubing, and cozy après in the village.

🚵

Summer

The Whistler Mountain Bike Park, alpine hiking, paddling on Alta and Lost Lakes, golf, and long patio evenings. Sunshine-season Whistler is a playground for all ages.

🍽️

Village & Wellness

A pedestrian village packed with restaurants, cafés, shops, and spas — perfect for slowing down between adventures, celebrating, or unwinding as a group.

Handled · Transport

Getting Everyone There

From the YVR arrival to the last shuttle home, we arrange the transfers so your guests never touch a rental-car counter or a mountain road.

✈️

From Vancouver (YVR)

Whistler sits about 120 km north of Vancouver International Airport — roughly a two-hour drive along the spectacular Sea-to-Sky Highway.

🚐

Transfers & Shuttles

Airport shuttles, private transfers, and group coaches make the trip up easy — no rental car or mountain driving required for your guests.

🚶

Getting Around the Village

Whistler Village is pedestrian-friendly and car-free at its core, with local transit and lifts close at hand — most days you'll barely need a vehicle.

We'll help you sort transfers and getting around as part of planning your trip.
